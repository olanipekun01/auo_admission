WORLD-CLASS FACILITATION

The University can boast of uninterrupted power and water supply, world-class facilities, modern library, as well as outstanding Engineering workshops and Laboratories for all our Academic Programmes, this indeed stands us out any day.

MEDICAL FACILITATIONS

The University has a modern NHIS accredited Medical Centre that is a one-stop shop for all health needs. It is equipped with state of the art equipment that renders diagnostic, screening, curative and counseling services in the shade of inpatient and outpatient services.

INTERNATIONAL STUDENT COMMUNICATION

The University has students from over 15 countries across the globe studying in various programmes. This has helped to create an abode of refreshing ambience that serves as a departure from the norm. Within the purview of systemic harmony, the University is a masterpiece of modern world-class architecture that is second to none in any Nigerian private University.

Collaborative LINKAGES AND CERTIFICATIONS

All the 23 programmes offered in the University have been fully accredited not only by National Universities Commission (NUC) but also relevant professional regulatory bodies such as COREN, CPN, ICAN, CIBN, etc. Apart from our linkages with NASRDA, IITA, NCAM, NIHORT, our students have access to professional certification/membership like, NIM, ACCA, CISCO, MICROSOFT, ORACLE, NSE etc.

The graduates of our Achievers University are christened "Pathfinders".
