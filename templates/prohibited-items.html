Security Measures

The university maintains strict security protocols to ensure the safety and well-being of all students, staff, and visitors. Regular inspections and searches may be conducted at entry points and within campus facilities.

  • All bags and personal belongings are subject to inspection
  • Metal detectors and security scanners are in operation
  • CCTV surveillance is active throughout the campus

Reporting Violations

Students are encouraged to report any violations of these rules to the appropriate authorities. Anonymous reporting is available through the following channels:

  • Campus Security Office: +234 8168064525
  • Student Affairs Office: +234 7038505649
  • Email: security@achievers.edu.ng

Consequences of Violations

First Offense: Written warning and confiscation of prohibited items
Second Offense: Suspension from campus activities and mandatory counseling
Third Offense: Temporary suspension from the university
Serious Violations: Immediate expulsion and involvement of law enforcement

Need Clarification?

If you have any questions about these prohibited items or need clarification on any specific item, please contact the Student Affairs Office.

Office Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location: Student Affairs Building, Ground Floor
