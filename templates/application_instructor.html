GUIDELINES FOR COMPLETING THE ONLINE PAYMENT AND APPLICATION FORM

1

Visit our Admission application portal via http://admission.achievers.edu.ng/app to complete the application payment form.

2

Complete the application payment form with the necessary and accurate details. Please note that your UTME registration number, a valid email address and GSM number will be required.

3

After completing and submitting the application payment form, a preview of the application is displayed. Verify its authenticity and correctness, then click on "Pay" button to make payment.

4

You will be redirected to the payment page where you are expected to make payment of ₦10,000 application fee along with a processing charge depending on the payment channel selected. Please note that you will need an ATM Card (Verve, Visa, MasterCard or E-Tranzact Card) to complete your payment.

5

After successful payment, you will receive a confirmation email with your application details and reference number. Keep this information safe for future reference.

6

Log back into the application portal using your credentials to complete the full application form with your personal details, academic history, and programme choices.

7

Upload all required documents as specified in the document checklist below. Ensure all documents are clear, legible, and in the accepted formats (PDF, JPG, PNG).

8

Please note that there will be POST-UTME screening exercise, which will take place at a later date to be announced. Further information will be communicated via the email address and phone number supplied when filling the application fee form.

PLEASE NOTE: You are expected to upload the following documents and come along with the original copies and photocopies of all necessary documents at scheduled Post-UTME screening exercise

Printed Application Form
Copy of UTME Result Slip
Copy of 'O' Level results
2 Passport Photographs
Original copy of sponsorship letter from Parents/Guardian
Letter from your religious leader
Copy of Birth certificate
State of origin certificate

Important Notes

  • Ensure all information provided is accurate and truthful
  • Keep your application reference number safe for future correspondence
  • Application fee is non-refundable
  • Incomplete applications will not be processed
