Getting to Campus

By Road

The university is easily accessible via the Owo-Benin Road. From Akure, take the Owo road and continue for approximately 30 minutes.

Distance from major cities:

  • • Akure: 45 minutes
  • • Benin City: 2 hours
  • • Lagos: 4 hours

Public Transportation

Regular bus services are available from major cities to Owo. From Owo town, take a taxi or motorcycle to the university campus.

Airport: Akure Airport (45 minutes drive)
